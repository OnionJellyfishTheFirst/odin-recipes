Best Ever Muffins

Description

These easy to make muffins are absolutly delicious! They can be made in as little as 35 mins. You can even experiment by adding your own special ingredients.

Ingredients

To make these muffins, you will need:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspon salt
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

How to make

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees C (400 degrees F). Grease a 12 cup muffin tray or line cups with paper liners.

  2. Stir the flour, baking soda, salt, and sugar together in a large bowl; make a well in the centre.

  3. Crack the egg into a small bowl and beat. Whisk in the milk and oil. Pour all of the egg mixture into the flour mixture; mix quickly and lightly with a fork until the flour mix is just moistened, creating a lumpy batter. Fold in your additional ingredients (see variations below). Spoon the batter into your prepared muffin cups, filling each cup 3/4 of the way.

  4. Place the muffins in the preheated oven and bake for about 25 minutes or until the tops spring back when lightly pressed.

Variations

  • Blueberry Muffins: Add 1 cup fresh blueberries.
  • Raisin Muffins: Add 1 cup finely chopped raisins.
  • Date Muffins: Fold in 1 cup finely chopped dates.
  • Cheese Muffins: Fold in 1 cup grated sharp yellow cheese.
  • Bacon Muffins: Fold in 1/4 cup crisp bacon, broken into bits.
